About me

Hi! I'm Leo, a designer with over 10 years of experience transforming ideas into digital products. My journey began in graphic design, evolved into digital marketing, and solidified in UX/UI, where I've specialized my focus on creating user-centered experiences. For the past 2 years, I've been working as a Product Designer, leading the design of solutions that directly impact business operations and productivity.

I've worked across diverse contexts: agencies, startups, and freelance projects, which has given me a comprehensive view of the digital product lifecycle. Currently, I'm part of the team at GeoVictoria, a SaaS technology company specialized in human capital management, where I design solutions that help over 7,000 clients across 20 countries optimize their HR processes.

My transition into Front End development is a natural evolution of my career. I've always worked hand-in-hand with development teams, which has allowed me to deeply understand the relationship between design and code. I'm currently completing a Front End Development bootcamp, mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while learning React and C#. Additionally, I've developed personal video game projects in code, which reinforces my ability to materialize ideas from concept to implementation.

My goal is to integrate design and development to have full control over digital product creation, building experiences that not only look good but function optimally and scale effectively.
